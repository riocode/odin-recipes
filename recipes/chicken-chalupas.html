Chicken Chalupas

Description

Chalupas are small, boat-shaped flatbreads that can be filled very simply, or can be served with all the usual toppings for a taco. Choose your own toppings, or use any or all of the suggestions and enjoy this south-of-the-border treat!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 1/4 cups whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 2 cups chopped iceberg lettuce
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese, or to taste
  • Optional toppings, such as pico de gallo, sour cream, or sliced avocados, to taste
  • 1 cup refried beans
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 2 tablespoons taco seasoning
  • Oil
