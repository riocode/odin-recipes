Honey Glazed Chicken

Description

Honey glazed chicken is a light meal that everyone can enjoy. It is better served with steamed rice. A great dish for family holidays!

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ⅛ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 ½ tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces
